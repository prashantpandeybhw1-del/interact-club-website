About Our Club

Service Above Self

The Interact Club is a Rotary International sponsored service club for young people ages 12-18. We are dedicated to developing leadership skills while serving our community and promoting international understanding.

Our Mission: To provide opportunities for young people to work together in a world fellowship dedicated to service and international understanding.

Founded: 2020
Members: 45+ Active Students
Projects Completed: 25+
